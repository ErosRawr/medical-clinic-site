Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare services for you and your family

NovaCare Clinic offers a full spectrum of medical services designed to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our community. Our experienced team of physicians and healthcare professionals are dedicated to providing quality care in a comfortable, patient-centered environment.

Primary Care

Our primary care services provide comprehensive, ongoing healthcare for patients of all ages. We focus on preventive care, health maintenance, and the management of acute and chronic conditions.

Core Services:

  • Annual physical examinations and health screenings
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, asthma)
  • Acute illness treatment and urgent care
  • Immunizations, vaccinations, and preventive care
  • Specialist referrals and care coordination
Schedule Primary Care Visit

Pediatric Care

Our pediatric department provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. We understand that children have unique medical needs and require a gentle, compassionate approach.

Core Services:

  • Well-child visits and developmental screenings
  • Childhood immunizations and health monitoring
  • School and sports physical examinations
  • Treatment of childhood illnesses and infections
  • Adolescent medicine and nutritional guidance
Schedule Pediatric Visit

Women's Health

We provide comprehensive women's healthcare throughout all stages of life, from adolescence through menopause and beyond. Our services emphasize preventive care and patient education.

Core Services:

  • Annual gynecological exams and wellness visits
  • Pap smears and cervical cancer screening
  • Breast health evaluations and mammography referrals
  • Family planning and contraception counseling
  • Menopause management and hormone therapy
Schedule Women's Health Visit

Laboratory Services

Our on-site laboratory provides comprehensive diagnostic testing with quick turnaround times. Having laboratory services in-house allows for efficient diagnosis and treatment planning.

Common Tests:

  • Complete blood count and metabolic panels
  • Cholesterol and lipid profile testing
  • Diabetes monitoring (A1C) and thyroid function
  • Urinalysis and infectious disease screening
  • Rapid testing (strep, flu, COVID-19)

Results: Most tests processed within 24-48 hours. Access your results through our secure patient portal.

Physical Therapy

Our physical therapy department helps patients recover from injuries, surgery, and manage chronic pain conditions. We develop individualized treatment plans to restore function and improve quality of life.

Treatment Focus:

  • Post-surgical rehabilitation and recovery
  • Sports injury treatment and prevention
  • Orthopedic conditions and joint replacement rehab
  • Back and neck pain management
  • Balance training and fall prevention programs
Schedule PT Evaluation

Urgent Care

For non-life-threatening illnesses and injuries that require prompt attention, our urgent care services provide convenient walk-in care with extended hours. No appointment necessary.

We Treat:

  • Minor injuries (cuts, burns, sprains, fractures)
  • Fever, flu symptoms, and respiratory infections
  • Allergic reactions and skin conditions
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Other non-emergency medical concerns

Urgent Care Hours

Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.
